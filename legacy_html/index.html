ABOUT JCOM

Welcome to JCOM

JCOM (Jaycees Chamber of Commerce / Junior Chamber Organisation for Members) is a dynamic leadership and business development platform that empowers individuals to grow personally, professionally, and socially. Through structured training, business networking, community service, and leadership opportunities, JCOM nurtures confident leaders who create positive impact in society.

Who are we?

JCOM is one of the world’s largest youth organizations of young leaders, entrepreneurs and active citizens with nearly 2,00,000+ active members and millions of alumni in more than 6,000 communities in over 120 countries.

Member Benefits

What You Get as a Member

20-Chapter Meetings Per Year: Our 90-minute meeting agenda provides a platform to invite qualified guests for the benefit of other Members, ensuring time and energy is invested.

Professional Business Growth: Enables every JCOM member to grow their business volume with confidence.

Systematic Networking: Provides networking platform with structured and systematic meetings.

Dedicated Mentorship: Handholding approach to ensure the business growth of member.

Strategic Collaboration: Connections with members across the country for collaboration to expand your business.

Expert Training: Business knowledge and skill development trainings with experts.

Who can Join?

Any individual (Professional/ Self employed/ Entrepreneur);
who is either active or associate/SMA or alumni member of JCI, can join JCOM through proper application.
