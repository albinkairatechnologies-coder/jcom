FAQ
Who is eligible to join?

Business owners, entrepreneurs, corporate managers, and professionals aged between 18 and 40 who are eager to grow their networks and serve the community.

How long does review take?

The Zone Governing Board typically reviews applications and updates candidate members within 5 to 7 business days.

Is there an interview process?

Yes, candidate members are invited to attend a JCOM chapter breakfast meet or call for a quick mutual introduction.
